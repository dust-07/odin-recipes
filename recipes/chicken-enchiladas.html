Chicken Enchiladas

Description

These chicken enchiladas are a comforting, crowd-pleasing dish featuring tender shredded chicken rolled in corn tortillas, smothered in a savory red enchilada sauce, and topped with melted cheese. They are perfect for a quick weeknight dinner that feels like a treat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken (rotisserie chicken works perfectly).
  • Tortillas: 8-10 corn tortillas.
  • Sauce: 1 can (15 oz) red enchilada sauce.
  • Cheese: 2 cups shredded Mexican blend or cheddar cheese.
  • Aromatics: 1/2 small onion, finely diced.
  • Seasoning: 1 tsp cumin and salt/pepper to taste.
  • Optional: Fresh cilantro or sour cream for garnish.

Steps

  1. Preheat and Prep: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Prepare the Filling: In a medium bowl, combine the shredded chicken, half of the cheese (1 cup), diced onion, and cumin. Add a splash of the enchilada sauce (about 1/4 cup) to keep the filling moist.
  3. Warm the Tortillas: To prevent cracking, lightly warm the tortillas in a microwave for 20 seconds or quickly sear them in a dry pan for 10 seconds per side.
  4. Assemble: Spoon a generous amount of the chicken mixture onto each tortilla, roll them up tightly, and place them seam-side down in the baking dish.
  5. Cover and Bake: Pour the remaining enchilada sauce evenly over the top of the rolls. Sprinkle with the remaining cup of cheese.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 15 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 5–10 minutes until the sauce is bubbling and the cheese is melted and slightly golden.
  7. Serve: Let cool for 5 minutes before serving. Garnish with cilantro or sour cream if desired.
